Our Products

Lal
Thecha

If you call yourself a spice lover, wait till you taste this “Lal Thecha”, it is sure to set your pants on fire! If you’re a brave-heart who loves some adventure with food then this thecha, the spiciest Indian chili sauce, is definitely for you. Abundance of flavor from the garlic, crunch from the peanuts, the use of red chillies compliments rice and dal (lentils) like nobody’s business and also goes very well with roti and dosa too.

Green
Thecha

It is a delectable spicy combo of green chillies, garlic, peanuts and corriander. It is a traditional Maharashtrian accompaniment and goes best with Chawal Bakhri, whole wheat bakhri and jowar bakhri.








Lasun Kanda Chutney

It is a zesty blend of onion & garlic to add spice & flavour to your dish and is amust for cooking Maharashtrian cuisines. It is used for preparing range of spicy vegetables with or without gravy. One can also use it as a seasoning with snacks like vada pav, roti spread, thalipeeth, bhaji, pakodas, etc




Solapuri Shengdana Chutney

Solapur is a place in Maharashtra, from where the recipe of this chutney comes. Shendana chutney is a must-have condiment in Maharashtrian kitchen. It adds such zing to the meal with it’s nutty flavour and comforting taste.
